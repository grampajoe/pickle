Catch the pickles!

Pickles are falling from the sky! Well, there isn't really a sky, just a big, purple void. So anyway, pickles are falling from the upper part of a purple void, and you need to catch them for some reason!

Look out for knives!

Whatever's spontaneously creating pickles has decided to drop knives, too! They'll break your jar, so fill it up and get a new one that isn't all busted up!
